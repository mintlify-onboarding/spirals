1. 1.  
Click "Connect Wallet" on Spirals dApp

2. 2.  
Choose "Other Wallets"

3. 3.  
Click "Copy to clipboard"

4. 4.  
Navigate to your Celo Wallet in a separate browser

5. 5.  
Click "More" then "Connect"

6. 6.  
Paste from your clipboard then click "Connect" then "Approve"

7. 7.  
Return to the Spirals dApp - in the top right corner it should now show your wallet address

Now you're all ready to start staking for climate impact! Great work.
